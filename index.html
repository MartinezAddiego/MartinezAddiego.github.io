ABOUT ME

I'm a fifth-year PhD candidate in Neuroscience and Cognitive Science at Georgetown University. I am co-mentored by Drs. Ella Striem-Amit (Sensory and Motor Plasticity Lab) and Maximilian Riesenhuber (Lab for Computational and Cognitive Neuroscience). My dissertation research leverages functional neuroimaging and behavioral measurements to understand the underlying representation of actions in the brain. Ultimately, we hope that our findings may contribute to advancing brain-computer-interface technology.

I am passionate about open science and technology, and I am always looking for opportunities to grow my skills.
